Problems with current app:

1. Users lose motivation:
– People download the app, buy OBD-reader and then never use the app. – People use the app some time and then lose the motivation. – People get offended when they get a bad score and as a result they become less motivated to use the app.
2. Users don't understand the app:
– People don’t understand how to start first ride and just close the app. – Results are not clear. – They don’t understand how to install OBD, it looks complicated.
3. Users are not encouraged:
– Users forget to turn on the app while they are driving. – They never upload photos of their car, which is very relevant in case of an accident.

User Portrait
Let's look at Michael

Michael is a project manager in an IT company. He saves money for a new apartment and at the moment rents a house with his girlfriend outside from the city. He often spends time in a gym and with his friends. He has a car, which he uses to commute to work every day.

Goals

He wants to save money and pay less for the insurance.

Frustrations

Can’t pause the ride if he went for 5 minutes to the shop. He wants to control how is riding while he is riding, but can't see live update of the score. It is not clear that you can start ride while phone is connecting.

User Scenario

Michael is interested in adjusting his budget to achieve his personal goals. He uses his car every day but there are no solutions which can help him to optimize permanent spendings on his car.

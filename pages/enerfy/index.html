Enerfy

An app which uses artificial intelligence to lower the price of your car insurance

Problems

Users lose motivation - they download the app and never use it. Some users thinks the app is not clear and that it's hard to understand. Users are also not encouraged to use it.

Typical user

Who is the typical user of the Enerfy app?

User Portrait

Let's look at Michael
Michael is a project manager in an IT company. He is saving up money for a new apartment and at the moment rents a house with his girlfriend outside the city. He often spends time in the gym and with his friends. He has a car, which he uses to commute to work every day.

Goals

He wants to save money and pay less for his insurance.
